frequently asked questions

If you still have questions not mentioned below, just let us know
What is Tierra City?
Tierra City is a DeFi metacity based on Solana Blockchain.
What is NFT?
Non-fungible tokens or NFTs are a unique and non-interchangeable unit of data stored on the Blockchain.
Will there be a pre-sale?
Yes, we appreciate the important people for the community and our early supporters, information about the whitelists will be published just before the Mint.
How does the reward system work?
Very simply, a holder who has a digital asset in his wallet can receive passive income from it.
I'm new to Solana, how can I participate?
You will need a Solana-based wallet (e.g. Phantom) which you can use to participate in Mint, store your assets and much more
